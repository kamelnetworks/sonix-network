Openness and Transparency

Our commitment to open-source technology fosters innovation, collaboration, and community ownership.

We operate with full transparency, ensuring that our partners and the public have clear insight into our operations and finances.

As a non-profit, our focus is on serving the community to connect Sweden’s internet infrastructure globally, not on generating profit.

Sustainable Infrastructure

Sonix is a Green IXP committed to sustainable practices. Our core network runs on open-source software and refurbished hardware, minimizing e-waste and reducing our environmental footprint.

By partnering with Sonix, you’re not just getting access to a high-performance network;
you’re also supporting a greener future.

High-Performance Connectivity

We provide robust and reliable connectivity solutions designed to meet your unique needs.

With 8 strategically placed locations across Sweden, we offer a range of port speeds from 10G to 400G, with all network equipment utilizing NOS SONiC.

Our flexible options include a variety of optics (including free 100G optics!), cross-connects tailored to your requirements, and the possibility for custom solutions.
